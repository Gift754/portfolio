About

I go by Gift, a name that stuck long before I knew I'd spend my days shaping how people experience the digital world.

I'm a UI/UX Designer with over three years of hands-on experience, currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at Strathmore University in Nairobi. That intersection, design sensibility meeting engineering thinking, shapes everything I make.

I've collaborated with Wamara Studio on real-world platforms that needed to earn trust fast and work beautifully. I care about the quiet details: spacing that breathes right, flows that need no explanation, interfaces that get out of the way.
